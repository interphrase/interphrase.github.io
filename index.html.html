The problem

Why most language learning fails professionals

Generic courses

Teach vocabulary nobody uses at work. Grammar tables instead of real professional communication — you study for years and freeze on your first work call.

App-based learning

Builds habits, not fluency. No professional scenarios, no real conversation. Duolingo streaks don't survive your first international meeting.

Passive study

Without profession-specific context, language knowledge doesn't transfer to real-world use. You know the grammar but can't speak your field.

You don't need more lessons. You need the right cognitive framework — built around your profession, your goals, your brain.

Our methodology
The Interphrase Method™

Developed at the intersection of applied linguistics, cognitive neuroscience, psychology and profession-specific immersion. Every program is built on peer-reviewed research — not intuition or tradition.

01

Neuroplasticity-Based Design

Sessions structured around your brain's optimal language-acquisition windows. Adults learn faster with the right cognitive triggers.

Based on: Merzenich, Doidge — adult neuroplasticity research
02

Comprehensible Input Protocol

Language is acquired through meaningful professional context — not memorized. Every session uses real scenarios from your field.

Based on: Krashen — Input Hypothesis
03

Spaced Repetition Architecture

Vocabulary reviewed at scientifically optimal intervals — maximizing long-term retention with minimum cognitive load.

Based on: Ebbinghaus — forgetting curve & spaced practice
04

AI-Augmented Curriculum

Each learner receives a personalized AI-assisted program — adapted to profession, pace, and cognitive profile. No two programs are identical.

Powered by: AI corpus analysis + Task-Based Language Teaching
